Introduction and A short Story About Myself

My name is Lify Gamardien Rotty, a student from Manado State Polytechnic, majoring in Electrical Engineering, specifically in the Informatics Engineering program.

I am an 18-year-old female student, born and raised in Manado City. Currently, I reside in Bilangan with my parents. I obtained my diploma in tourism business from State Vocational High School 3 Manado. Presently, I am pursuing a D4 degree in Informatics Engineering. Despite my parents' preference for majors such as Mining Engineering, Petroleum Engineering, or Industrial Engineering, I discovered that those fields did not align with my passions. Consequently, I decided to pursue my long-standing dream of studying Informatics Engineering, a passion I've nurtured since junior high.

The disparity between my vocational school and university majors highlights the shift from tourism business to informatics engineering. During my vocational high years, I opted for the tourism business not out of genuine interest but due to circumstances I choose not to elaborate on here. Despite my initial lukewarm enthusiasm for the subject, I embraced it with zeal.

Upon entering university, I decided to follow my true passion Informatics Engineering. This choice was not solely driven by personal interest but also by recognizing the pivotal role of information technology in our digital age. I acknowledge that studying informatics engineering offers me opportunities to delve into and contribute to technological advancements.

While my vocational school major may have initially been a misstep, it taught me valuable lessons about accountability and seizing opportunities. I view it as a chance for holistic learning, encompassing not only informatics engineering but also leadership, perseverance, and adaptability in diverse situations.
